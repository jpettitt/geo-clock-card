GeoClock — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

The short version: GeoClock has no accounts, no analytics, no trackers, and no servers of our own. We never collect, receive, store, or sell your data. Everything runs locally in your browser. This policy covers the GeoClock New Tab Chrome extension and the geoclock.world website.

What we collect

Nothing. GeoClock has no back-end that receives information about you. We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising identifiers, fingerprinting, or third-party analytics.

Settings stored on your device

Your preferences — map center, location markers, day/night marker colors, language/locale, and the hour-band / timezone / UTC toggles — are saved in your browser's local storage (localStorage) so they persist across new tabs. This data stays on your device, is never transmitted anywhere, and you can clear it at any time by resetting the settings in the Customize panel or clearing the extension's site data.

Your location

The browser geolocation API is used only if you choose to use it — by selecting "center on my location" or adding a "my location" marker in the Customize panel. When enabled, your coordinates are used in your browser to position the map and are never stored in a shareable link, never saved to our servers (we have none), and never sent to any third party. If you never enable those options, GeoClock never requests your location.

Place-name search

When you add a marker by typing a place name, the text you type is sent to the OpenStreetMap Nominatim geocoding service to look up its coordinates. Only the text you enter is sent; no personal data, location, or identifier accompanies the request. This feature is optional — if you add markers by coordinates or by your current location, no request is made. Nominatim is operated by the OpenStreetMap Foundation under its own privacy policy.

Map imagery and timezone data

All NASA Blue/Black Marble imagery and the timezone boundary data are bundled inside the extension and load locally. Displaying the map requires no network connection and makes no external requests.

Permissions, explained

  • Geolocation — used only for the optional "center on my location" / "my location" marker features described above.
  • Access to nominatim.openstreetmap.org — used only for the optional place-name search described above.

GeoClock requests no other permissions. It does not read your browsing history, tabs, bookmarks, or the content of any web page.

Children's privacy

GeoClock collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "last updated" date above will change and the revised policy will be posted at this URL.
